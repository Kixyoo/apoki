Having started small as a YouTuber in 2019, APOKI successfully made her official debut as the very first virtual K-POP artist with the first digital single ‘Get It Out’ in February 2021. Shortly after, APOKI started gaining a massive popularity throughout all social media platforms, which has awakened the music industry to the potential of ‘virtual artists’ to be as successful as human artists. APOKI’s fourth single ‘West Swing feat. E-40’ released in August 2022, featuring the legendary rapper E-40, has gathered a huge fandom mainly across South Korea, Japan and North America. The fifth single ‘Mood V5’ released in February 2023 has attracted a lot of attention globally, thanks to the collaboration with Sony Honda Mobility’s ‘AFEELA’ prototype in the music video. APOKI has amassed over 5 million social media followers worldwide and is now known as one of the most influential virtual musicians in the world. Her songs are created by multi-platinum, award-winning producers and songwriters, and the music videos are solely produced by the cutting-edge technology of AFUN Interactive Inc. and its many experts.
